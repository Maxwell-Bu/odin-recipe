Delicious Anything Tempura

This recipe will show you how to make the most delicous fried food. of your life. Please make it now. If you don't I will cry.

Ingredients

  • Any Object
  • Oil
  • Egg
  • Flour
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Add flour to a sheet tray along with a pinch of salt
  2. Beat some eggs and put in sheet tray
  3. Put your object into the flour and press the flour tightly into it
  4. Wet flour-covered object in egg wash
  5. Repeat step 3
  6. Heat some oil to 350 and place your object into it
  7. Fry until GBD (Golden Brown Delicous)
  8. Enjoy!
